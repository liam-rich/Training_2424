**Product:** Clinical dashboards for small hospitals (aggregated metrics, not raw EMR replacement).

**Constraints:**

- Regulators need **auditable requirements** and **verification evidence** before **live PHI** connections.

- **Team:** 8 people, co-located, cross-functional skill mix.
- **Commercial:** Potential **fixed-price, frozen-scope** **18-month** rollout to **40 sites** after pilot phase.
