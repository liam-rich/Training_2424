# Instructor notes — Agile vs Waterfall case (not for trainees)

**Acceptable answers** typically:

- **Phase A:** Agile/Scrum (learning, uncertainty) **plus** lightweight but **real** compliance discipline (living requirements, versioned specs, trace matrices for regulated slices).
- **Phase B:** Often **more Waterfall-like gating** per release train **or** **SAFe-like** PI planning—**not** “pure Waterfall forever,” but **stronger** baseline and **change control** for contract.

**Red flags:** “Agile means no documentation” or “Waterfall means no feedback.”

**Discussion prompts:** Cost of late change in regulated data paths; **Definition of Done** including evidence; **PO** role with compliance officer as **stakeholder**.
